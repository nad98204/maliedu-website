# Checklist QA Truoc Khi Dang Bai (Ban Cap Nhat)

Su dung checklist nay truoc khi bam "Dang bai" trong Admin Posts.

## A. Kiem Tra Noi Dung

- [ ] Tieu de ro rang, dung intent tim kiem
- [ ] Mo bai nói dung van de va loi ich nguoi doc nhan duoc
- [ ] Co it nhat 3 heading phu (H2/H3) de de doc
- [ ] Co vi du/checklist huong dan, khong chi ly thuyet
- [ ] Co ket luan + CTA cuoi bai
- [ ] Khong loi chinh ta, cau qua dai, lap y

## B. Kiem Tra SEO

- [ ] Slug gon, khong dau, co tu khoa chinh
- [ ] SEO Title nam trong 50-60 ky tu
- [ ] SEO Description nam trong 140-160 ky tu
- [ ] Tu khoa chinh co trong title + phan dau noi dung
- [ ] SEO Keywords da nhap 5-8 cum lien quan
- [ ] Co it nhat 1-2 internal links den bai/trang lien quan
- [ ] Da su dung helper dem ky tu trong admin (title/description dang mau xanh)

## C. Kiem Tra Anh Va Media

- [ ] Anh bia da upload thanh cong (LongVan S3)
- [ ] Da nhap Alt anh bia co y nghia va lien quan noi dung
- [ ] Anh khong mo, khong vo khung, kich thuoc hop ly
- [ ] Neu la bai video: videoUrl hop le

## D. Kiem Tra Admin Form

- [ ] Category dung voi chu de bai viet
- [ ] Type dung (Bai viet / Video / case-study)
- [ ] Author da dung chuan thuong hieu
- [ ] Da bam "Xem truoc" va doc lai layout
- [ ] Neu chua san sang: tat "Hien thi ngay" de luu nhap
- [ ] SEO Keywords da them theo tag (Enter hoac dau phay), khong de 1 cum duy nhat
- [ ] Editor Tiptap hien thi dung format sau khi paste noi dung AI

## E. Tieu Chuan Dat Dang Bai

Chi dang bai khi tat ca muc A, B, C, D deu pass.

Neu co 1 muc fail:
- sua truoc trong form
- bam xem truoc lai
- moi tiep tuc dang bai

## F. Kiem Tra Tinh Nang Tuong Tac Trang Chi Tiet

- [ ] Co loi moi de lai chia se trai nghiem (feedback section)
- [ ] Co form newsletter va test gui email hop le
- [ ] Co section "Doc tiep bai lien quan" hien thi it nhat 3 bai
